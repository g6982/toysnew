Odoo POS Cash Control

Control your cash efficiently in running Odoo POS!

Odoo POS Cash Control allows you to manage the cash controls in the running POS session. This means, you can keep the entries of taking out and keeping money in the cash register. Moreover, you can also set the closing cash and track the payments.

Cash management is one of the most crucial parts for a business to look into as improper management can lead to several losses. When you run a store, you need to track each and every transaction made so that transparency stays to calculate the return of investment.
A healthy cash control management ensures that all the transactions are properly managed and avoids chaos. Proper cash control also helps in analyzing the cash flow which is important to stay on top of changing cash trends. If you are using Odoo POS to run your offline store then you don't need to worry about cash control.
Now, enhancing the existing default feature of Cash Control in Odoo POS. Odoo POS Cash Control not just helps you to manage cash flow; but also facilitate you to manage efficiently in running POS. You can manage the record of keeping the cash, taking out cash, set closing cash and track payment; all in running POS sessions.
Manage the activities done with the Cash Box in Odoo POS

Being human, you cannot remember every detail; hence, it is necessary to keep track of everything that is important. The module allows you to manage the cash in and cash out hassle-freely. Though you can do the same in default Odoo POS as well.

What extra our module Odoo POS Cash Control offers than default?

The default flow is perfect; however,by default, to make the entries of cash in and cash out, you need to close the session. Now, you can make these entries in the running session.

Hence, it is time saving as well as reduces the risk of not remembering to make the entries. Moreover, the entries made from POS sessions can be tracked from the backend as well.

Easy way to Keep Track of Payments in running Session of Odoo POS

Analyzing the transaction helps you to know the areas which are profitable or which are less profitable comparatively. The module saves you from switching in between POS sessions and its backend.

You can simply keep the track of Payments made in POS from the running session only. There is a separate section for payments which you can easily track.
