Odoo Website Store Locator

Let the Customers Locate your physical Stores via Google Map!

Odoo Website Store Locator facilitates your customers to reach your physical store following the map. You can set your store locations in Odoo which your customers can check from the frontend without any hassle.

Customers ease is one of the most important factors to ensure more potential buyers to your website. Even when you are running an online store, still there can be many reasons when your customers need to reach your physical store. Moreover, many people prefer seeing the products in real, before buying them online, so for them it is beneficial to look for your store.

However, the challenge begins when the customers cannot find your store easily. Hence, you need to look for a way so that your customers can easily locate your store without any hassle. A google map location tracker can help people to easily find the location.

Now, if you are running your website in Odoo then you can facilitate your customers to locate your physical store. Odoo Website Store Locator module allows you to share your physical store location on your website.

Show your store location to your customers in Odoo Website

Now, you can show your store location on the Odoo Website without any hassle. You just need to set map settings by choosing map centre either to Auto or manually option. You can also select the desired type of map which could be roadmap, satellite, hybrid and terrain.

Moreover, you can also define desired map zoom index, select desired address option which could be either address or coordinate. Set desired search radius.

The module also facilitates you to activate/deactivate the defined desired location settings on the Odoo website.
